What is GoCardless?

GoCardless lets businesses easily collect payments online, directly from your customers' bank accounts (rather than using expensive card networks). Bank payments are a pull payment method, putting the business in control of triggering the payment. The customer just needs to authorise it one time, and the job is done.

You can use GoCardless to set up automated and recurring payments using Direct Debit. You can also collect collect instant, one-off payments.

All of our payment processing uses bank payments. Leaving cards behind means you enjoy lower fees, easy automation, and reduced payment failures, saving you time, stress, and money.

How do I use GoCardless?

There are three different ways you can use GoCardless:

  • Log into our online dashboard (easiest) – Use GoCardless as a standalone way to collect payments.
  • Connect to your existing software – And collect payments with GoCardless that way. 350+ systems are supported, including Xero, QuickBooks, and Zuora.
  • Integrate via our API – Create a custom experience for your customers

You can initiate one-off instant payments and start collecting regular payments through any of these options. None of the options are better or worse than each other – it's up to your preference and business needs.
